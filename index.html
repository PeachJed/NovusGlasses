Learn More About Novus Glasses

With a different look for each and every person, these glasses offer a unique design for every day.

  • Novus Glasses are a brand of high-quality, prescription eyewear made with Italian acetate, polycarbonate, and silicone. The company has been creating these sunglasses since 2008, with the name translating to “new” in Latin.
  • Novus Glasses are a revolutionary pair of glasses that allow you to read up to 40% faster and 40% more efficiently than traditional reading glasses.
  • Novus Glasses are eyeglasses with a sleek and modern design. They have a polarized lens, a semi-rimless frame, and a plastic hinge that allows for the glasses to be folded. They also come in a variety of colors and sizes.

When you're searching for a pair of new glasses, it can be overwhelming. There are so many options, and you'll have to make a choice based on your lifestyle and your personal style. What's more, it's difficult to know what you'll be getting when you buy them. Are they really the best quality? Are they really made for you? Are they really worth the money? The solution is to shop for glasses online. Here, you can search for a specific style or brand and find out all the important information about them before you even buy them. You can also compare the cost of different frames and find the perfect one that will fit your budget. The main downside of shopping online is that you won't be able to try on your glasses. However, that's easily remedied with the assistance of a professional. You can go to a local optometrist or ophthalmologist and they'll help you find the right pair of frames for you. Online shopping has many benefits, such as easy accessibility and instant feedback. You'll know right away if the glasses are the right size and fit you properly, or if they're not a good fit for your face.

History

Know Our History With Us

Established

Novus Glasses was founded by a few people in New York who saw the need for a quality eyewear company that catered to the customer. They wanted to provide people with a unique pair of glasses that wasn't just fashionable, but also offered quality and affordability. Novus Glasses started in the streets and slowly gained popularity in the fashion industry. They have now expanded to offer their glasses through the internet and at select retailers across the United States.

Named

The glasses are named Novus because they are different from the regular glasses. They are a different style, more up-to-date, and more appealing to the eye.

Designed

Novus is a new brand of glassware with a completely modern twist. With these glasses, you'll never have to worry about your glasses fogging up, breaking, or getting lost again. The Novus frames are made of a high-quality plastic that has a unique shine to it that is perfect for any occasion. The best part? These glasses are so light and durable that you'll barely even notice you're wearing them.

Price

Price of Novus Glasses are in a league of their own. Each pair is handmade and custom-fitted to the wearer's unique prescription.
